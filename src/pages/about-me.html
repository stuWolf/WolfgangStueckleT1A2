Who Am I?

I lean into change and take the opportunity to grow and learn from every experience. I am a creative thinker who likes to be challenged by diverse thinking and varied work. I enjoy achievement and getting results, often with my eye on the big-picture goals, over the more minor details and tasks. I move at pace and can sometimes make decisions in the moment, not always having all the information. I see continuous improvement and professional development as essential to be successful in the fast paced ever changing environment.

My long term goal is to achieve either a masters in AI or a masters in biomedical engineering. I am fascinated by both especially the work on the brain computer interface by the likes of Synchron or Neurolink.
