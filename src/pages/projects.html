Development of a new ECM function package from customer specification

In the last decade, the engine management software developed to a fairly complex system consisting of more than 450 functions directly involved in the engine management itself plus another couple of hundred functions on the driver and BIOS layer. This results in a total of about half a million lines of C- code. This is far more than the computer programme of the space shuttle consisted of. It takes a building full of engineers to maintain and develop a system of this size. This complexity is handled by a strict definition of module interfaces and processes how a new software module has to be developed, tested and released. (please see figure 1) Currently, a team of over a hundred software engineers are dealing with the latest generation engine management software internal called ME9 (Motronic Electronic throttle), spread in workgroups and each workgroup deals with one specific function group. For example, one workgroup develops the knock control functions for all customers and another one is responsible for the driver and BIOS layer and so on. This kind of work split is certainly the key to handle such a complex system. The disadvantages are however that the development time from specification to release to the customer often takes unacceptably long and the effort for communication between the workgroups is considerable.

TASK TITAN Full stack application

TASK TITAN, deployed version

The purpose of this app is to provide a platform for customers and trades people to initiate trade jobs, assign them to workers and track their progress. Also give customers an option to leave a review.

Tech stack: React, Node and express JS, MongoDB, monghoose

Features:

User Authentication

  • User sign up with email verification
  • User sign in
  • JWT authentication
  • Encrypted password (bcrypt + salted) stored in database

User Roles and Views

  • Different views for business owner/manager, workers, and customers

Job Management

  • Customers can initiate new jobs and request quotes
  • Managers can quote on jobs and assign workers
  • Workers can accept jobs and mark them as completed

Notification System

  • Notifications for new jobs, job assignments, changes in job status, and new reviews

Review System

  • Customers can sign off on jobs and leave reviews

Search and Filter

  • Search and filter jobs, tradespeople, or reviews
  • Manager Functions
    • Receives notification for new jobs and quotes requested by customers
    • Views all open jobs
    • Quotes on jobs
    • Receives notifications when quotes are accepted
    • Assigns workers to jobs and refines job descriptions
    • Receives notifications when jobs are completed
    • Reviews and closes jobs

    Worker Functions

    • Receives notifications for new job assignments
    • Accepts jobs
    • Marks jobs as completed
    • Views jobs assigned to them

    Customer Functions

    • Raises new jobs and requests quotes
    • Receives notifications when quotes are issued
    • Accepts quotes and gives go-ahead
    • Signs off on jobs and leaves reviews

    General Functions

    • Users must log in to lodge jobs or reviews
    • Jobs can be canceled by the user or admin
    • Admin can view all users
    • Users can update their details or delete accounts
